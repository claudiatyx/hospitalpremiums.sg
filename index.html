Affordability strategies

🏦
Medisave top-up fund
Start regular voluntary top-ups to Medisave now. Maximum $66,000 tax-relief per year. Earns 4% p.a. and can be used to pay IP premiums directly.
💰
Dedicated sinking fund
Set aside a lump sum in a dedicated cash reserve at age 65 to fund cash outlay from age 66 onwards. Target 20–25 years of projected cash shortfall.
⬇️
Step-down at retirement
Downgrade from private to Ward A or B1 at age 65 when restructured hospital care is generally sufficient and premiums drop significantly.
📋
CPF LIFE optimisation
Choose the CPF LIFE Escalating Plan to get payouts that increase ~2% p.a., helping offset premium inflation in later years of retirement.
🔄
Rider removal at 65
Removing riders at 65 reduces total premium significantly while retaining base IP coverage. Pair with a cash reserve to self-insure co-payment risk.
📈
Start planning early
For a client aged 40, a monthly savings of $300–500 into a low-risk endowment or Singapore Savings Bond can build the target sinking fund by age 65.
